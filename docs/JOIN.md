# Copy-paste blurbs for group messages

Plain-language snippets for inviting folks to join. Each code block
below has a copy button — click it and paste straight into the chat.

---

## Short pitch (always start with this)

```
Jackson Film Club has a public member directory now: jxnfilm.club

Joining just takes an email — Letterboxd is optional. If you do have
Letterboxd and link it, your profile shows up next to your name and you
can add which screenings you've been to.
```

---

## How to join (no Letterboxd needed)

```
How to join Jackson Film Club:

1. Go to join.jxnfilm.club
2. Enter your name and email. Leave the Letterboxd field blank.
3. Check your email for a 6-digit code (from noreply@join.jxnfilm.club —
   check spam if you don't see it).
4. Enter the code on the page that opens. That's it — you're a member.

You can always add a Letterboxd profile later from the "Edit account"
page if you decide to make one.
```

---

## How to join (with Letterboxd)

```
How to join Jackson Film Club + link your Letterboxd:

1. Go to join.jxnfilm.club
2. Enter your name, email, and your Letterboxd handle (just the
   username, no @).
3. Check your email. You'll get TWO things in one message:
   - A 6-digit code to confirm your email.
   - A short verification tag that looks like jxnfc-verify-XXXXXX.
4. Enter the 6-digit code on the page that opens. You're now a member.
5. To link your Letterboxd profile, paste the jxnfc-verify-... tag
   into either:
   - A diary entry's review/notes field, OR
   - A new list on your profile.
   Then go to jxnfilm.club/edit, paste the URL of that diary entry or
   list into the form, and click "Verify Letterboxd". Once it matches,
   your @handle shows up on the directory and you can delete the
   verification tag from Letterboxd.

The tag is good for 48 hours. If it expires, you can request a fresh
one from the same Edit page.
```

---

## After you join — getting credit for screenings

```
Getting marked as "attended" on a Film Club screening:

If you've linked your Letterboxd, it's automatic:
- Log the film in your Letterboxd diary on the date of the screening.
- Add the tag "jxnfilmclub" to that diary entry.

The site refreshes every few hours and you'll show up on the event's
attendance list. No app, no check-in — just tag your diary entry.

No Letterboxd? You can self-report attendance from the event page
after you've signed in.
```

---

## Common questions

```
Q: Do I need a Letterboxd account to be a member?
A: No. Email is all that's required. The Letterboxd link is purely
   optional and only exists so people can find each other's profiles.

Q: Can I remove my Letterboxd later?
A: Yes — go to jxnfilm.club/edit and there's a "Remove Letterboxd link"
   button. Your membership stays; just the @handle disappears.

Q: Where does my email go?
A: Stored privately for sign-in only. It's never shown on the public
   directory. Full policy: join.jxnfilm.club/privacy.
```
